What is GDPR?

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a European Union regulation that came into effect on May 25, 2018. It gives EU/EEA residents greater control over their personal data and sets out strict rules for how organisations collect, store, and process personal data.

Football Zone is committed to GDPR compliance. This page explains how we handle the data of EU/EEA users in accordance with the regulation.

Lawful Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, we must have a lawful basis for processing your personal data. The following table outlines our processing activities:

Processing Activity Lawful Basis
Displaying advertisements (AdMob) Consent (you can opt out)
Processing Premium subscription Contract performance
App analytics & crash reporting Legitimate interest
Local app data (favorites, settings) Contract performance / Legitimate interest
Daily notifications (optional) Consent (you enable/disable in Settings)
📦

Data We Process

  • Advertising Identifier (GAID) — used by Google AdMob for ad personalization.
  • Device information — device model, OS version for compatibility purposes.
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active Premium subscription (via Google Play).
  • App interaction data — wallpapers liked, downloaded, and set (stored locally).

We do not process sensitive personal data (health data, biometric data, etc.) and we do not build user profiles for sale to third parties.

International Data Transfers

Some data may be processed outside the EU/EEA (e.g., by Google AdMob servers in the United States). These transfers are protected by:

  • Google's compliance with the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework.
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.

Your GDPR Rights

As an EU/EEA resident, you have the following rights under GDPR:

👁️

Right of Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

✏️

Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

🗑️

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

⏸️

Right to Restriction

Request that we restrict processing of your data.

📤

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

🚫

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.

↩️

Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., disable ad personalization).

⚖️

Lodge a Complaint

File a complaint with your national data protection authority (DPA).

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at support@dovdovdev.com. We will respond within 30 days.

🍪

Consent for Personalized Ads

When you first launch Football Zone, we request your consent for personalized advertising through Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP). You can change your consent preferences at any time:

  • In your Android device settings: Settings → Google → Ads
  • By contacting us to withdraw consent

If you decline personalized ads, you will still see ads, but they will not be tailored to your interests.

📅

Data Retention

  • Local data (favorites, collections, settings) — retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • Advertising data — retained per Google AdMob's retention policy (typically up to 2 years).
  • Subscription data — retained for the duration of your subscription and as required by Google Play.
